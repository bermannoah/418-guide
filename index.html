HOW TO MAKE A GOOD COFFEE

(at turing)

  • 1. Notice that the coffee pot is empty.
  • 2. Clean the vacuum pot. Clear grounds from basket, spigot.
  • 3. Grind coffee
  • 4. Put filter in basket
  • 5. Put coffee in filter. Wet (lightly, carefully) with hot water.
  • 6. Place basket into brewer. Add carafe.
  • 7. Press go button.
  • 8. Smell sweet coffee smells. Oh, that sweet coffee smell.
  • 9. When brew cycle finishes, write brew-time on carafe. Insert straw-thing, close.
  • 10. Pour yourself a cuppa. Enjoy. Then empty the grounds basket and clean the spigot.
